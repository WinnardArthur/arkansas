About the LAPS SYSTEM

The Lean-Agro-Process Support System, a digital technology that provides commodity specific technologies, with associated economic and environmental information and decision modelling, to advise farmers and small scale Agro-processors on the best intervention strategies to maximize profit and reduce post-harvest losses.
